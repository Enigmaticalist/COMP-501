The Moral Implications of White Hat Hacking in Cybersecurity.

White hat hacking references ethical hacking practices, where individuals use their skills and/or abillities to identify and fix security vulnerabilities in systems, with the consent of the system owners. This proactive approach helps protect against malicious attacks and promotes cybersecurity.
